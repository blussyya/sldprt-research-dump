## Results

All 17 tested invariants pass 100%. Zero structural violations.

## Critical finding: INV-012 formula is incorrect

INV-012 documents `len = 2 * loopSize - 2`. Substituting `loopSize = (raw + 2) / 2` gives `len = raw`. Corpus data: 0/3429 sections match. Correct relationship is `len = raw - 1` (INV-017).
