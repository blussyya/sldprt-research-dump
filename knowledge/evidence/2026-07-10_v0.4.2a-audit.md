## Audit 3: INV-018 mathematical independence

**Finding: NOT independent.** INV-018 follows from INV-017 + section splitting definition.

Proof:
1. From section splitting: `sum(sectionLen) = b1len - secs`
2. From INV-017: `sectionLen[i] = b2[i] - 1`
3. Substituting: `sum(b2[i] - 1) = b1len - secs` → `sum(b2) - secs = b1len - secs` → `sum(b2) = b1len`

Verification: 1232/1232 faces where INV-017 passes also pass INV-018. Zero counterexamples.

## Audit 4: INV-012 discrepancy

**Finding: Documentation mistake.** INV-012's formula `len = 2 * loopSize - 2` reduces to `len = raw`. Corpus data (8763 sections): 0 match `len = raw`, 8763 match `len = raw - 1` (INV-017). Correct constant is -3, not -2.
